2. Mini Cheese Balls

Ingredients:

  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Chopped herbs
  • Crushed nuts

Steps:

  1. Mix cheeses and herbs together.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Roll in crushed nuts.
  4. Refrigerate before serving.
