Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in cocoa and flour.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 20–25 minutes.

2. Fruit Custard

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • Chopped fruits (apple, banana, grapes, mango)

Steps:

  1. Mix custard powder with a little milk.
  2. Boil remaining milk with sugar.
  3. Add custard mix and stir until thick.
  4. Cool and add chopped fruits before serving.
